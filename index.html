About EAR, Inc.

EAR, Inc. is the global leader in custom and universal-fit hearing protection. For over 50 years, we’ve crafted precision silicone earplugs and accessories for shooting, music, sleeping, swimming, and industrial use. Our network of 2,500+ providers worldwide ensures perfect fits anywhere.

Hearing is irreplaceable. From custom electronic earplugs to universal-fit instant protection, we use the same advanced silicone technology across all products.
