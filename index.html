About EAR, Inc. – Global Leader in Custom & Universal Hearing Protection

EAR, Inc. is recognized worldwide for delivering high-quality custom and universal-fit hearing protection that enhances the hearing experience, supports hearing healthcare professionals, and drives innovation in the industry. With certifications for Europe, USA, and Australia, our products meet the highest global standards for safety and performance.

Our custom-molded silicone earplugs provide unmatched comfort, superior noise reduction, and proven protection against hearing loss. Trusted by the United States Military, law enforcement, motor sports enthusiasts, and shooting/hunting professionals, EAR custom-fit solutions — including electronic, acoustic-filtered, and passive earplugs — excel in functionality, hygiene, and long-term value.

Complementing our custom line, EAR offers a full range of universal-fit earplugs and earmuffs using the same advanced technology found in our molded products. From medical-grade TPE electronic earplugs for all-day comfort to expandable slow-recovery foam disposables, our universal solutions deliver reliable, instant protection for industrial workers, musicians, swimmers, and everyday users.

Through our Industrial Hearing Protection Program, our team — with over 45 years of field and manufacturing expertise — provides on-site corporate fittings and tailored OSHA-compliant solutions. Meanwhile, our audio and music earplugs are the choice of professional musicians and audiophiles worldwide, preserving sound clarity while preventing hearing damage.

For over 50 years, EAR, Inc. has led with innovation, quality, and a network of 2,500+ certified providers globally. Whether you need custom earplugs for sleeping, swimming, music, or industrial use — or universal-fit instant protection — EAR delivers hearing protection you can trust.
