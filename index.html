About EAR Customized Hearing

Founded in 1971, EAR Customized Hearing has over 50 years of experience manufacturing premium custom and universal earplugs. Trusted by the U.S. Military, law enforcement, musicians, and recreational enthusiasts, we provide certified solutions worldwide through 2,500+ independent providers.

Who We Serve

  • Musicians & audiophiles
  • Hunters, shooters, & motorsports enthusiasts
  • Swimmers & watersports participants
  • Sleepers and general hearing protection users
  • Military, first responders, and tactical teams
  • Children & pediatrics
